## ⚙️ Mechanics

### Energy vs Capacity
- **Energy:** Joule (J), kJ, MJ.
- **Magical capacity:** **erg** (dimensionless in physics; tracks modulation endurance).

### Conventions
- Costs are split into:
  - **Modulation cost (erg)** — stress on mineral/apparatus.
  - **Fuel cost (J)** — energy drawn from chemicals/heat/electricity/Tain.

### Examples
- Ignition of 1 L oil: modulation 30 erg, fuel ~ 35 MJ (scene-scale).  
- Emission-channel beam (3 s): 12 erg modulation + whatever fuel source provides.
